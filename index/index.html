OUR SPEAKER

Dr. Ankur Sodhi

Associate Director, Career Services & Corporate Relations

Dr. Ankur Sodhi is working with upGrad Education Private Limited, the pioneer in Online Learning space. In his work here as Associate Director, Career Services & Corporate Relations, he get the opportunity to touch lives of students and working professionals all across the country. This opportunity to make a wider impact is an extremely satisfying experience for him. Earlier, till November, 2019, He was working with Lovely Professional University since 2007 and designated as the Head of School of Professional Enhancement. In this proliferating journey, in 2010, Dr. Ankur Sodhi was promoted to the post of Coordinator of the Department which includes several pivotal administrative tasks and responsibilities. Along with this, He also work as Deputy Director in Division of Admissions, Placement Coordinator for the Department of Computer Science and now an Associate Professor, specializing in courses on Soft Skills, Team Building Skills & Networking. Currently, he has a team of more than 100 trainers who work towards making the students industry-ready.


For almost 12 years now, as an educationalist, public speaker and strategic planner, he has played a vital role in improving systems and functions of the institutions and organizations that he has been a part of to behoove students and create a smooth transition for them from the educational world to the professional world.
